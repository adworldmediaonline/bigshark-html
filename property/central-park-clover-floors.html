Overview

Clover Floors in Sector 33 Sohna, Gurgaon is meticulously designed with unbound convenience and the best of amenities and are an effortless blend of modernity and elegance. The development is a residential project by Central Park. Central Park is a well-known property development and management company which emphasizes on customer fulfillment.
The builders of Clover Floors understand the aesthetics of a perfectly harmonious space called Home that is why the floor plan of Clover Floors offers unique blend of spacious as well as well-ventilated rooms. With the right blend of technologies in its design, the intelligent homes are equipped with smart facilities including voice control devices and panic buttons. The master plan comprises of unique design that affirms a world-class lifestyle and a prestigious accommodation in Apartments in Gurgaon.
Last but not the least the area is quite the good connectivity. The development is 35 Minutes from IGI Airport, 20 Minutes from Central Park Resorts and 10 Minutes from Golf Course Extension Road. Located in the foothills of Aravalli, the area has retail, educational and healthcare facilities in the vicinity.
