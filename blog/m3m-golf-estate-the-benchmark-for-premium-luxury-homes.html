  • Posted10 Mar 2023

While there are many top-notch residential projects in Gurgaon and NCR, only a few of them have the best floor plan. M3M Golf Estate is one of them. The M3M Golf Estate floor plan underscores the 3BHK + 3T servant home model, and it involves three exciting options based on distinct spacious arrangements. Apart from this feature, Bigshark Developer, the developer of the project, has also added several other advanced features that highlight its thoughtful planning and execution.

Bigshark Developer is a reputed real estate developer in Gurgaon and NCR with a brilliant track record of completing flagship residential and commercial projects with success. From the choice of location to the availability of the amenities, it has equipped M3M Golf Estate with all the top-notch features that assure profitable returns on investment to both potential homebuyers and real estate investors.

How M3M Golf Estate Floors meet the needs of Luxury Users

From traditional homes, most homebuyers are switching to contemporary dwellings in the luxury segment these days. There are many reasons that define this approach. To begin with, luxury homes, such as the ones at M3M Golf Estate at Sector 65 in Gurgaon, bring the choicest comforts under a roof. In the last few years, there has been an unprecedented rise in the demand for such homes among homebuyers in Gurgaon. The feature-rich homes of M3M Golf Estate are capable of satisfying it.

As a luxury resort-styled housing project, the M3M Golf Estate 3 BHK Floor Plan sets the trend for luxury living with a lavish lifestyle. Spread across an area of 56 acres of land; it offers 3 and 4 BHK residences with the promise of premium comfort. While it boasts a long list of noteworthy features, the one which is the best for most buyers of homes in Gurgaon is the availability of huge indoor spaces. Together with this feature, several other elements make the homes of M3M Golf Estate an attractive investment option for both homebuyers and real estate investors.

When it comes to the availability of different types of homes, M3M Golf Estate is second to none in Gurgaon. When booking your home under this project, you can choose an option from many exciting possibilities. These include a penthouse, a 3BHK Den, a 4 BHK, or a 5 BHK residence. As regards its floor plan, it lets buyers choose their pick from three distinct options under the 3BHK + 3T servant home model with the following space options:

●    3203 sq. ft.
●    3452 sq. ft.
●    3469 sq. ft

For individuals who are inclined to the design features and appearance of luxurious residents, M3M Golf Estate presents peerless architecture and design. All of its homes are well-distributed among towers of distinct heights. Based on the thoughtful planning of a prestigious Canadian company, it offers luxurious residences with stunning views and arguably the best ventilation arrangements among planned townships in Gurgaon and NCR for the health and well-being of the residents.
