Elan The Presidential Sector 106 Gurgaon An Overview

  • Posted27 Dec 2022

Exploring Elan The Presidential Sector 106, Gurgaon

Gurgaon has become the epitome of India’s growth. Gurgaon, the neighbouring city of Delhi drew the attention of corporates. World-class infrastructure, better connectivity and wide scope of development made Gurgaon an ideal corporate centre of attraction. As a result, several elite industrial families made their abodes here. As Gurgaon had a remarkable growth spurt, the demand for luxurious housing increased manifold. 

Several renowned builders launched luxurious projects to address these demands. Elan The Presidential Sector 106 Gurgaon is one such project that meets the unexpressed needs of the ultra-rich clientele.

Details of the Elan The Presidential Sector 106 Gurgaon

As the name suggests, Elan Presidential is an ultra-luxury project that is designed by keeping the unique needs of the elite class. Elan builders earned a big name in the commercial segment. However, Elan The Presidential is the first residential project of the company. 

Through Elan The Presidential, Elan builders have expressed their vision to address the unspoken expectations of high-end clients. Therefore, the project is going to be best-in-class, ultra-modern, and the epitome of luxury with breathtaking architecture and world-class amenities.

The main features of the project are:

●    Elan The Presidential will come in sector 106 over 40 acres of land. The project will offer a feeling of openness as 60% of the project area will be green land.

●    The project's location is Dwarka Expressway, which will connect it with NCR and provide easy access to the airport.

●    Every luxury apartment will face central green lands. As a result, there is a calm, peaceful and scenic view from the rooms.

●    Spacious and fully air-conditioned rooms in 3/4/5 BHK options.

● Podium-level clubhouse with Olympic size swimming pool, gym, badminton and tennis court.
 

Why Elan The Presidential is a Good buy

Elan Presidential sector 106 is an ideal buy for high-end clients. This project meets all needs of an elite housing society. Effluent and higher-class gentry will constitute your neighbourhood. You will get all luxuries within the society periphery - an ideal way to compensate for your busy lifestyle.

Elan Presidential also makes the best investment bet among investors, if your aim is rentals. As Gurgaon is the hub of several big corporates, many CEOs and foreign nationals stay in the city for a limited number of years. These people wish to stay in a high-class society that can provide them with all facilities in proximity, have good airport reach and neighbourhood is also good that can form a good liaising platform.  Therefore, you will surely earn good rentals on your investment.
