Joyville by Shapoorji Pallonji Fits Well on Most Parameters

One housing brand that takes care of all your needs is JOYVILLE GURGAON at Sec 102, Dwarka Expressway, by Shapoorji Pallonji. A trusted brand gained much popularity owing to its commitment and reputation in the real estate market. After much success of Phase 1, it has come with the launch of Phase 2 and Phase 3 with the layout plan of 2 & 3 BHK apartments.

Joyville Gurgaon: An Ideal Choice for Housing and Investment Purposes
Let's now see what makes JOYVILLE Gurgaon the best choice for stay and investment.

The Project is spread over a sprawling area of 18 acres with 13 towers and 1560 apartments. The Project focuses on residents' privacy, comfort and security. To achieve all this, the builders have incorporated the following. 

Features that Make Joyville Gurgaon by Shapoorji Pallonji an Eye Candy

●    All the apartments are powered by Jio Fiber, giving all the residents uninterrupted internet backup 24*7, so your professional and personal growth doesn't stop due to poor internet connectivity. 
●    The size of the flats varies from 915-2180 sq feet, and each flat has its private terrace garden.

Surprised? Of course, you would be. Once a lifetime, you meet this opportunity of owning a private terrace garden, a feature mostly available with villas.

●    75% project area is open, which adds a touch of nature.
●    Gas leak sensors and video doorbell -JOYVILLE Gurgaon has always been famous for its active and updated safety features.
●    True to its name, JOYVILLE truly brings joy to kids with 3 colourful parks on-campus
●    Gym, Spa, Clubs, Party Halls, a Swimming Pool, Playgrounds and much more scope to rejuvenate after a long and hectic day. 
●    The house's flooring has been done using vitrified, textured, and wooden laminated tiles. All the sanitary fittings have been done from Jaguar, along with a suitable heating arrangement using geysers and solar heaters. The heart of the home - the modular kitchen; is a treat for the eyes.
●    The levels of pollution are rising in and around NCR. The situation is getting worse with each passing day. To address this issue, the Project has more than 70 air purifiers at all vital places of the Project.
