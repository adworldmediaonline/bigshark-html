Bigshark Developer is a professionally managed real estate services, property brokerage and consulting company based out of Gurgaon/Gurugram which provides a wide array of services down all the verticals which includes Commercial real estate sales ( Retail space, Anchor stores, Shops, Showrooms, Food Court, Office Space, Serviced Apartments, Cinema/Multiplex screens), Residential property sales( Apartments, Builder floors, Villas, Independent houses, Penthouses, Row houses, Luxury Condominiums), Affordable housing, Leasing services, Asset management Services and Strategic Consulting.

Bigshark Developer is the brainchild of extensive hermitage and in-depth research of Real estate investment and consulting business by the founders. Bigshark Developer is the end of the query for the clients who are looking for a companion rather than an agent.

We firmly believe that understanding the client's decision making heuristics precisely is the only key to surpass the client expectation and enhancing customer satisfaction. Today, although the modern infrastructure in India is at par with the international standards which provides almost all the basic amenities required by the customers, but every individual or organization has different business or need specific requirements and that's where Bigshark Developer comes into the picture. 

We understand in depth the requirement of our clients and look for a tailor-made solution by analyzing all the possible parameters like location, connectivity, accessibility, feasibility, viability, futuristic overview, return on investment, developer credibility, financial aspects and many more such parameters.
